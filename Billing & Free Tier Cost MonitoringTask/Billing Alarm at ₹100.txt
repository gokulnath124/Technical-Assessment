
1.Go to AWS Console → search "CloudWatch"

2.In CloudWatch dashboard, click Alarms → Create Alarm

3.Choose Metric → Billing → Total Estimated Charge

4.Select Currency: INR

5.Set:

      Threshold: ≥ 100 INR

6.Add action:

      Send notification to an SNS topic (Email required)

7.Name it:

      BillingAlarm100INR

8.Click Create Alarm
